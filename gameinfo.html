Game Rules

  • Bacteria cafeteria is a clicker game, where user keeps clicking to keep advancing.
  • User must sign in to play the game.
  • Germs will appear on the hand and will multiply every 10 seconds.
  • To kill germs user should click on it.
  • As more germs appear health bar goes down.
  • To stay alive user must click on the germs to kill them, doing so will earn him points.
  • User can use points to buy different abilities from the shop to kill more germs.
  • When user is logged off(not playing), germs will still continue to multiply and health bar will deteriorate continuosly at a certain rate.
  • To stay alive user needs to keep health bar from reaching 0 by frequently playing the game.
