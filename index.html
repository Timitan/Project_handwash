Bacteria Cafeteria Game Info









Bacteria Cafeteria is a clikcer game that shows some of the effects and behaviors of germs that may come into contact with your hand. User clicks on the hand on screen full of germs to kill them and to earn points. They can use those points to buy different abilities to kill germs more efficiently. If they stay away from game for too long germs multiply and user loses. It's purpose is to give user this idea that washing their hands frequently can help them prevent any sickness or infection.
